An Outline of Dr. J.S. Rajkumar

At the heart of medical innovation and compassionate care, Dr. J.S. Rajkumar has dedicated his life to advancing the field of surgery and improving patient outcomes. With over three decades of experience, Dr. J.S. Rajkumar is a pioneer in laparoscopic surgery in India and has introduced numerous innovative techniques that have transformed surgical practice.

Driven by his strong belief in the importance of giving back to society, Dr. Rajkumar founded the ‘Dr. Rajarathinam Medical and Educational Foundation’ in 1997. The foundation is dedicated to assisting patients suffering from HIV by covering their medical expenses and supporting the education of underprivileged individuals.
